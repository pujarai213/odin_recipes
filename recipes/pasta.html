Creamy garlic and pasta

Home

Description

A rich and creamy garlic pasta made with simple ingredients. This recipe is perfect for busy days and can be prepared in under 30 minutes. It’s comforting, flavorful, and loved by all ages.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 2 tbsp grated cheese
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and add chopped garlic. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add cream and milk, then mix well on low heat.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper, and chili flakes.
  5. Add the cooked pasta and toss until evenly coated.
  6. Sprinkle grated cheese and mix gently.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
