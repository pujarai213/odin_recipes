Veggie Cheese Omlette

Home

Description

A quick and healthy omelette loaded with fresh vegetables and melted cheese. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a light dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp chopped capsicum
  • 2 tbsp chopped tomato
  • 2 tbsp grated cheese
  • 1 tsp oil or butter

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add milk, salt, and pepper. Beat well.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add onion, capsicum, and tomato. Saut√© lightly.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  5. Cook until the base sets, then sprinkle cheese on top.
  6. Fold the omelette and cook for another minute.
  7. Serve hot with bread or salad.
