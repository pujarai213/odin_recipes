No-Bake Chocolate Biscuit Cake

Home

Description

A super easy dessert made without baking. This chocolate biscuit cake is rich, chocolaty, and perfect when you crave something sweet quickly.

Ingredients

  • 200g plain biscuits (broken into pieces)
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup butter
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat milk, sugar, cocoa powder, and butter in a pan. Stir until smooth.
  2. Turn off the heat and add biscuit pieces and nuts. Mix well.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a greased dish or tray.
  4. Press evenly and let it cool.
  5. Refrigerate for 1–2 hours until set.
  6. Cut into pieces and serve.
